★ "HORROR"? What does it all mean?
☆ horology, the scientific study of measuring time and making of instruments to keep time (which is our mission, obviously) + horror vacui, the fear of empty space (which is our design philosophy) = us.

★ What's with all the double-V?

★ Explain the skull.
☆ That's Mort, our little mascot. He's here to remind everyone that time flies, that life is fleeting, so seize the day.
