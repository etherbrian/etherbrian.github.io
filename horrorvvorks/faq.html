Why “HORROR”?
horology, the scientific study of measuring time and making of instruments to keep time (which is the mission here) + horror vacui, the fear of empty space (which is my design philosophy) = this. *waves hands around at everything*

Why the double-V in “VVORKS”?
When little French children recite their ABC’s, they pronounce the 23rd letter as “dooblah-vay”. It’s an ancient thing, and you can read more about it here.

What's with the skulls? Why “EARTH MOVT”?
The skull is Mort, the HORROR mascot, and EARTH MOVT is just a way of being less regional and more global. They're both an integral part of all the forthcoming HORROR VVORKS VVATCHES. They also appear on all the various other samples featured here simply to demonstrate my ability, my mission, my passion to make certain that the branding of anyone with whom I collaborate will be carefully, intentionally, pleasingly incorporated.

What exactly is the point of all this?
This place exists as a collection of wristwatch design ideas, mostly for demonstrating to potential collaborators what it is that I do. Also, though slightly less so, I'm showing the world what's to come with my own brand.

Which do you prefer, mechanical or quartz?
That's like asking a parent which child they love more.

What's your background?
I've been an illustrator, graphic designer, iconographer, and all-around Art Guy for a long, long while. You may have seen my work elsewhere without realizing it was mine. Hopefully, you want to see more, and you can via my portfolio.

How does one get in touch with you?
Let's start with an email. Once we get a thing going, we'll unleash all the other communication thingies!
